This is WalkEEG

WalkEEG is an open source system of Brain-Computer-Interface.

It is designed for developing science research and providing high tech 'toy' for Makers.

Why WalkEEG?

You may know Neurosky or Emotiv.

But they aren't open source. Therefore, scientist or maker can not revise them to adopt their own design or application.

WalkEEG is opensource, both hardware and software. Furthermore, we provide recommended BCI headset design for 3D-print.
